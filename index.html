About Me

Hello!! I am a Software Engineering Undergraduate(Class of 2017) pursuing B.Tech degree from Indian Institute of Information Technology,Allahabad. I am passionate about Web Technologies, Computer Networking, Programming, Sports mainly Football and Action/Spiritual Movies and Motivation Shows.

I'm always looking to explore more and more. Learning and practising new technologies makes me happy and motivated. In my free time, I do things that enriches my knowledge. I am really glad you visited my website. Have a look around and do leave a message.
